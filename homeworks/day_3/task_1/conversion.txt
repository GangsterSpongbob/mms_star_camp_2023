Let's take the number 145
In Decimal: 1 4 5
            1 * 10 ^ 2 + 4 * 10 ^ 1 + 5 * 10 ^ 0

In Binary: 0 1 0 1 0 0 0 1
		   1 * 2 ^ 6 + 1 * 2 ^ 4 + 1 * 2 ^ 0	
		   Note: Signed variable would be 1 1 0 1 0 0 0 1

In Octal: 2 2 1
		  2 * 8 ^ 2 + 2 * 8 ^ 1 + 1 * 8 ^ 0

In Hexadecimal: 9 1
		        9 * 16 ^ 1 + 1 * 16 ^ 0
